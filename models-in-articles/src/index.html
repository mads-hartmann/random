Say we allocate $3.1 billion for the following program: Car-owners who trade in an old car that gets less than 17 MPG, and purchase a new car that gets better than 43 MPG, will receive a $3,500 rebate.

We estimate that this will get 857,143 old cars off the road. It will save 1,583 million gallons of gas (or 101 hours worth of U.S. gas consumption.) It will avoid 14.78 million tons CO2e, or 0.21% of annual U.S. greenhouse gas emissions.
